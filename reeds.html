Mission

Lone Pine Reeds strives to craft professional oboe and English horn reeds with a warm tone, stable pitch, and precise response in all ranges. Every reed is personally handmade by Luke Simonson to suit students, amateurs, and professionals.

Just beginning to play oboe?

So many students come into their first lesson with machine-made reeds that are absolutely unplayable, making oboe more difficult to learn. When students play on my reeds, they immediately get better results. Lone Pine Reeds provide ease of response, pitch stability, and a controlled tone, all of which will greatly aid the beginning oboist.
